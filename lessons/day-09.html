Day 9 Linear Equations & Graphing

Linear Equations Word Problems

Vocabulary

Starting Value
The $y$-intercept ($b$) in context: the value of the quantity when $x = 0$. Often described as a "base fee," "initial amount," or "starting balance."
Rate of Change
The slope ($m$) in context: how much $y$ changes per one unit increase in $x$. Expressed with the word "per" (per hour, per mile, per item).
Linear Model
An equation $y = mx + b$ that describes a real-world situation. Valid only when the rate of change is constant.
Define Variables
The first step in any word problem: explicitly state what $x$ and $y$ (or other variables) represent, including units.
Unit Rate
A rate whose denominator is 1, equivalent to slope. "$\$6$ per hour" means $m = 6$ dollars/hour.
Break-Even Point
The value of $x$ where two cost (or revenue) models are equal. Found by setting two linear equations equal to each other and solving.

Concept

Word problems become manageable when you follow a consistent setup process. The goal is to translate the problem's language into the form $y = mx + b$.

Four steps to model any linear word problem.

🔍
Spotting slope and intercept in word problems:
• Words like per, each, every, for each additional signal the slope.
• Words like initial, starting, flat fee, base charge, at time zero signal the $y$-intercept.
⚠️
Always check your answer by substituting back into the original context. If a plumber charges $\$50/hr + \$75$ base and you find cost after 4 hours, verify: $50(4) + 75 = 275$. Does that match the problem?

Worked Examples

Example 1 — Service Fee Context

A plumber charges a $\$75$ base fee plus $\$50$ per hour. Write an equation for total cost $y$ after $x$ hours. Then find the cost of a 4-hour job.

1
Define variables: $x$ = hours worked, $y$ = total cost in dollars.
2
Identify slope: "$\$50$ per hour" → $m = 50$.
3
Identify $y$-intercept: "$\$75$ base fee" (cost at $x = 0$) → $b = 75$.
4
Equation: $y = 50x + 75$. For 4 hours: $y = 50(4) + 75 = 200 + 75 = 275$.
$y = 50x + 75$; cost for 4 hours = $\$275$
Example 2 — Data Table Context

A town's population is shown below. Write a linear equation and predict the population in year 5.

Year ($x$)0123
Pop. ($y$)4000420044004600
1
Slope: $m = \dfrac{4200 - 4000}{1 - 0} = 200$ people per year.
2
$y$-intercept: when $x = 0$, $y = 4000$, so $b = 4000$.
3
Equation: $y = 200x + 4000$. At year 5: $y = 200(5) + 4000 = 5000$.
$y = 200x + 4000$; predicted population at year 5 = 5,000
Example 3 — Break-Even Context

Plan A costs $\$20$/month plus $\$8$ per class. Plan B costs $\$44$/month with unlimited classes. After how many classes do the plans cost the same?

1
Write equations: Plan A: $y = 8x + 20$.   Plan B: $y = 44$.
2
Set equal: $8x + 20 = 44$
3
Solve: $8x = 24 \;\Rightarrow\; x = 3$ classes.
4
Check: Plan A at 3 classes = $8(3)+20 = 44$ ✓ = Plan B ✓
The plans cost the same at 3 classes per month ($\$44$).

Practice Problems

A plumber charges a $\$50$ base fee plus $\$30$ per hour. What is the total cost for a 5-hour job?

$y = 30x + 50$. Substitute $x = 5$.
$y = 30(5) + 50 = 150 + 50 = \$200$.

A gym membership costs $\$40$ per month plus $\$10$ per fitness class. Which equation models total monthly cost $y$ for $x$ classes?

Rate per class = slope. The monthly flat fee = $y$-intercept.
$m = 10$ (per class), $b = 40$ (monthly base). Equation: $y = 10x + 40$.

A candle is 20 cm tall and burns at 2 cm per hour. After how many hours is it 8 cm tall?

Height decreases: $y = -2x + 20$. Set $y = 8$ and solve for $x$.
$-2x + 20 = 8 \Rightarrow -2x = -12 \Rightarrow x = 6$ hours.

A taxi charges a $\$3.00$ base fare plus $\$2.50$ per mile. What is the total cost (in dollars) for a 4-mile trip?

$y = 2.50x + 3$. Substitute $x = 4$.
$y = 2.50(4) + 3 = 10 + 3 = \$13$.

A table shows hours worked ($x$) and dollars earned ($y$): $x$: 0, 1, 2, 3  |  $y$: 12, 17, 22, 27. What is the rate of change?

Rate of change = $\dfrac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}$. How much does $y$ change each time $x$ increases by 1?
Each hour, earnings increase by $17 - 12 = 5$. Rate of change = $\$5$ per hour. The $y$-intercept ($b = 12$) represents a $\$12$ base payment.

A car starts 120 miles from home and drives toward home at 40 miles per hour. After how many hours is it 40 miles from home?

Distance from home decreases: $y = -40x + 120$. Set $y = 40$.
$-40x + 120 = 40 \Rightarrow -40x = -80 \Rightarrow x = 2$ hours.

A savings account starts with $\$500$ and grows by $\$75$ per month. After how many months does the balance reach $\$1{,}250$?

$75x + 500 = 1250$. Subtract 500, then divide by 75.
$75x = 750 \Rightarrow x = 10$ months.

A phone plan charges $\$30$ per month plus $\$0.10$ per text message. How many texts can you send if your budget is $\$50$ per month?

Set up: $0.10t + 30 = 50$. Subtract 30, then divide by 0.10.
$0.10t = 20 \Rightarrow t = 200$ text messages.

A city's population was 5,000 in year 2000 and grows by 200 people per year. In what year will the population reach 7,000?

Let $x$ = years since 2000. $200x + 5000 = 7000$. Solve for $x$, then add to 2000.
$200x = 2000 \Rightarrow x = 10$ years. $2000 + 10 = $ year 2010.

Plan A costs $\$20$/month plus $\$5$ per class. Plan B costs $\$35$/month plus $\$2$ per class. At how many classes per month do the two plans cost the same?

Set the two cost equations equal: $5x + 20 = 2x + 35$. Solve for $x$.
$5x + 20 = 2x + 35 \Rightarrow 3x = 15 \Rightarrow x = 5$ classes. Check: Plan A = $5(5)+20=45$, Plan B = $2(5)+35=45$ ✓
