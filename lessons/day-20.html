Day 20 Polynomials & Quadratics

Polynomials: Operations & Special Products

Key Vocabulary

Polynomial
A sum of terms of the form \(ax^n\) where \(n\) is a non-negative integer. Examples: \(3x^2 - 2x + 1\).
Degree
The highest exponent in a polynomial. \(3x^4 - x + 7\) has degree 4.
Leading Coefficient
The coefficient of the term with the highest degree.
FOIL
First, Outer, Inner, Last — a method for multiplying two binomials.
Perfect Square Trinomial
\((a+b)^2 = a^2+2ab+b^2\) and \((a-b)^2 = a^2-2ab+b^2\).
Difference of Squares
\(a^2 - b^2 = (a+b)(a-b)\). Only works with subtraction, not addition.

Special Products Reference

Difference of Squares
\(a^2 - b^2 = (a+b)(a-b)\)
Example: \(x^2 - 9 = (x+3)(x-3)\)
Perfect Square (Sum)
\((a+b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2\)
Example: \((x+4)^2 = x^2+8x+16\)
Perfect Square (Diff)
\((a-b)^2 = a^2 - 2ab + b^2\)
Example: \((x-5)^2 = x^2-10x+25\)

Worked Examples

Add: \((3x^2 - 2x + 5) + (x^2 + 4x - 3)\)

Group like terms:

\(x^2\) terms: \(3x^2 + x^2 = 4x^2\)

\(x\) terms: \(-2x + 4x = 2x\)

Constants: \(5 + (-3) = 2\)

Answer: \(4x^2 + 2x + 2\)

Multiply: \((x+5)^2\)

Use the perfect square formula: \((a+b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2\)

Here \(a = x\) and \(b = 5\):

\(x^2 + 2(x)(5) + 5^2 = x^2 + 10x + 25\)

Warning: \((x+5)^2 \ne x^2 + 25\)! Don't forget the middle term.

Multiply: \((3x-2)(3x+2)\)

Recognize the pattern: \((a-b)(a+b) = a^2 - b^2\)

Here \(a = 3x\) and \(b = 2\):

\((3x)^2 - 2^2 = 9x^2 - 4\)
